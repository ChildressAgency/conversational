Conversational FAQs

We Know you may have questions, especially if you're new to using a virtual receptionist

Below you will find the answers to some of our most frequently asked questions. If you cannot find the answer to your questions, you can contact us, and we will be happy to provide an answer!

Landlines

AT&T

  • Dial * 72 or 72# and listen for dial tone
  • Enter the area cod and number you want to forward your call to
  • Wait for the confirmation tone, after the tone the system will automatically place a call to the number. Once the forward to party answers the call the feature is activated.

~ If you encounter difficulties please reach out to your carrier to ensure your forwarding is enabled with your phone services ~
