Do you Really Need 24/7 Coverage?

Part of the reason Answer Connect charges a little bit more per minute is to offer 24/7 coverage. Even though you only pay per minute, they still have to staff their call centers during every hour of every day, which can be expensive.

Some businesses need 24/7 coverage, but not all businesses need this extended coverage. If you are a common 9am to 5pm business, Conversational will already help you expand your phone hours to 7am to 8pm. This gives you an added five hours per day, along with weekends and holidays. For some businesses, this is plenty and the added hours past 8pm or before 7am are simply unnecessary.

Paying a company a higher rate when you won’t need them during the late and very early hours may not really help you at all. It’s better to choose a company offering what you really need. If you don’t need after-hours emergency phone answering, Conversational may be the better choice.
