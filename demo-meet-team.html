Meet the Team

Our experienced and friendly team of receptionists are dedicated to your success

Conversational is the leading virtual receptionist and answering service in North America. Our team of skilled and friendly virtual receptionists are dedicated to delivering a great experience to your callers.

Through great customer experiences, our receptionists build trust to win you new business and foster your customer loyatly. We are proud to say our receptionists are valued employees, not contractors. And we value them as highly as we do our clients.

At Conversational, we are not just colleagues, we are family.

Tanya Lamont, President

Tanya Lamont President

Tanya brings to the team an unparalleled work ethic and commitment to quality customer service. Her friendly and positive attitude makes her the perfect member of the team to lead the charge for the company and ensure consistent, professional and friendly customer service.

With education in Business and over 20 years of Contact Center Experience, Tanya is an amazing asset to the Conversational brand. Her background experience in all forms of business whether it be Bookkeeping, Business Management, Marketing and Human Resources has been obtained through managing several industries directly; to include health care, legal, construction and beauty. With this experience she is adept in understanding the needs of Conversational’s clients and their industries ensuring delivery of a service that will grow with every business world wide. Mix that with a strong technical background and you have a dream leader that works to build her staff up, to become the best in their field in this virtual world we live in today.

Tawnya Thompson Sales and Operations Manager

With over 20 years of experience in supply chain, procurement, customer service and sales her skills have been a great asset to Conversational. She is also our beauty industry “Guru” and has helped with our success in servicing the beauty industries supported by our reception services.

Tawnya has performed many roles within the company through its growth and had found her calling working with the clients. She brings dedication and passion to the team and believes through support, growth, and commitment Conversational will become the leader in virtual services.

With over 28 years in receptionist service and management, Krista brings knowledge and respect of the virtual reception service with her to Conversational. Krista has shown terrific leadership and capable training abilities throughout her time with Conversational.
