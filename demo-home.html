The Right Virtual Receptionist or Answering Service

Ensure your company never misses another call as we answer your telephone and either transfer the call to you, perform basic customer service or take a message.

Features

Personalized Call Service for whenever you need it

We can set up your calls to route exactly how you wish. You can turn our service off and on when you need it - even if it's only during your busiest times, or when your receptionist is on vacation. With personalized greetings and hold music, we have you covered.

  • Live Call Answering

    Missed calls are missed opportunities! Your virtual receptionist will answer your incoming calls in a friendly and professional manner with a personalized greeting and call handling process you can fully customize.

  • Outbound Calls

    Outbound calls are offered at an additional cost with every virtual receptionist plan we offer. Have some outbound phone calls to make and a million reasons why you don't want to do it? Let us reach out to reschedule those appointments, collect information, or deliver information as per your request.

  • General Inquiries & Basic Customer Service

    Every question our receptionists can answer for your callers is one less call you'll have to deal with! With Conversational, providing basic customer service is one of our top virtual receptionist features!

  • Voicemail Messages Delivered by Email

    No matter where you are when a voicemail comes through, you have the ease of listening to your message on the go.

  • Advanced Call Screening

    You can say goodbye to intrusive calls from salespeople and telemarketers! Your virtual receptionist is adept in the art of call screening in a professional and friendly way.

  • Appointment Booking

    Our team can schedule, reschedule, or cancel appointments on your behalf, using your current scheduling software. And because your schedule will instantly show the changes made, you're always up to date.

  • Customized Call Handling

    We customize the call handling to fit your business. Each call type, staff member, or department can have their own unique set of call handling instructions, ensuring efficiency and ease of use for everyone.

  • Appointment Reminders

    Our appointment reminder service is one of our top-rated virtual receptionist features and has been shown to reduce no-shows by up to 75%. What could that do for your business?

  • Set Temporary Call Instructions

    For meetings outside of the office, emergency scenarios, or those afternoons you choose to get out and take in a round of golf, you can have peace of mind that you won't miss any important calls during your absence. Our Mobile app makes this even more convenient than ever.

  • One Way Secure Text Notifications

    Don't use email that much or do not have access to this on the go? No problem! Our receptionists can deliver messages via text messaging right to your cellular device. This feature is also offered through our Mobile App.
